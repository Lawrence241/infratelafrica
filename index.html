About Us

Welcome to Infratel Africa

…Connecting Africa, Transforming lives.

Communication has always been a part of our history and culture. We provide telecom collocation and infrastructure sharing, power as a service, telecom backhaul and enterprise security services, and rural transformation using technology.
Infratel Africa connects Mobile Network Operators, amongst others under a cooperative model, hence we can get Africa communicating more, doing more, growing more and unifying more.
